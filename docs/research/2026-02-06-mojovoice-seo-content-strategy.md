### 1.4 Voice Search Optimization

**Voice Search Characteristics (2026):**
- Average voice query: 4-7 words (vs. 2-3 for typed)
- 41% of voice search answers come from featured snippets
- 58% of voice searches have local intent (less relevant for MojoVoice)
- Voice answers average 29-40 words in length
- Growing intersection with AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity)

**MojoVoice Voice Search Opportunities:**
- "How do I use voice to text as a developer?" → Featured snippet opportunity
- "What's the best voice recognition for Linux?" → FAQ/Answer optimization
- "Can I run speech recognition locally without internet?" → Conversion query
- "How do I add voice commands to my Python app?" → Tutorial/guide query

**Voice Search Optimization Tactics:**
1. Create FAQ pages addressing developer pain points in Q&A format
2. Optimize answers for 29-40 word length (voice assistant response length)
3. Target conversational question keywords (who, what, where, when, why, how)
4. Build featured snippet-worthy content (concise, well-formatted answers)
5. Structure content for AI assistants (proper headers, lists, clear answers)
